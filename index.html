FeltMC is a project that will run Forge, Quilt, and Paper mods on Fabric. Our goal is to reach as close to 100% compatibility as possible on Fabric Loader, to make certain that just because two mods work in different environments, doesn't mean they can't be used together!

Want to try it out?

Because we Felt like it

Performance

We aim to load and run mods faster than their native environment, if possible, by trimming unnecessary bloat, and optimizing/rewriting mod loading implementations.

Community

We try to be welcoming to all people from all walks of life, so long as they don't mean harm to us or our project. Drop in anytime on Discord, Telegram, Reddit, Misskey, or Matrix!

Compatibility

We want to load all mods. Like many of you, we're tired of our favorite mods existing on different platforms with no interop at all. We aim to fix that once and for all, to the best of our ability!
